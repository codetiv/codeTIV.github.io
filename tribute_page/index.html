Potato Manufacturer

Most of us will know what is a french fry or potato chips are but do we understand what it's from or how it is even processed? Let us breifly dive into how it's processed.

  1. Wash: Clean off most the dirt.
  2. Peel: Takes off the skin.
  3. Blanch: Precook.
  4. Cut: Slice to desired pattern.
  5. Fry: Deep fry!

That list is the basic of what happens, it's easier said than done. Spark youtube video of their docu-series made a good video of this process, it is a bit over 22min long but if you are interested in it, there you have it!

Packaging

After all the cooking is done, this is where they start to package everything for the consumer in a nice pretty bag.

Besides the normal dishes we're all or most accustomed to, there are other resources during this process of potatoes we probably don't know.

The Byproduct

  • Adhesive
  • Animal Feed
  • Fuel

Starch is widely used ingredient to make adhesive for many types of industries, as the peels from potatoes can even create ethanol, and probably commonly will be used a feed for cattle.
